# Zero Budget Marketing Plan for Sarathi Book

**Objective:** Acquire the first 1,000 active drivers without spending any money on ads.
**Core Strategy:** Leverage "Word of Mouth" and existing driver communities.

## 1. The "Viral Invoice" Strategy (Passive Growth)
Every invoice sent is an advertisement. Your PDF invoices already have the "Sarathi Book" footer.
*   **Action:** Ensure every driver knows how to send a PDF invoice to their customer.
*   **Why:** Customers (who often book cabs) and **other potential drivers** (who might see the bill) will see the "Sarathi Book" branding.

## 3. Google Maps & Local SEO (Discovery)
*   **Optimize GMB:** Ensure "Sarathi Book" is listed on Google Maps if you have a physical office (or even a home office).
*   **Reviews:** Ask 5 close driver friends to write a 5-star review on the Google Play Store (if listed) or Google Maps listing mentioning "Best app for drivers".

## 4. YouTube Tutorials (Content)
Video is the best way to teach drivers.
*   **Create Short Videos:** Record your screen showing:
    1.  *How to make a bill in 1 minute.*
    2.  *How to calculate One-Way Drop fare correctly.*
*   **Distribution:** Upload to YouTube as "Shorts" and share on WhatsApp.
*   **Tags:** Use local language tags (e.g., "Digital Bill Tamil", "Taxi App Chennai").

## 5. Community "Help, Don't Sell" (Trust)
*   Join Facebook groups like "Drivers in Chennai," "Tamil Nadu Taxi Drivers."
*   **Do NOT just spam link.**
*   **Instead:** When someone asks *"How much to charge for Chennai to Bangalore drop?"*, calculate it using **Sarathi Book**, take a screenshot, and reply with the image saying: *"Here is the correct calculation using Sarathi App."*
*   **Result:** People will ask, *"What app is that?"* -> You win a user.

## 6. Physical "Guerrilla" Marketing (Offline)
*   **The "Chai Shop" Chat:** When you are at a tea shop or airport waiting area with other drivers, show them the app.
*   **Demo:** Open the app and calculate a fake trip for them. *"Look, it adds the Driver Batta automatically."*
*   **Install for them:** Help them add it to their home screen (PWA).

## 7. The Referral Loop (In-App)
*   **Feature:** Add a distinct **"Share with Friends"** button in the app dashboard.
*   **Incentive:** Even without money, the incentive is "Help your brother drivers."
